> I thought Mr. Keshav Prasad Maurya will take the seat because to me, he looked like a decent balanced person who would make peace.

> As soon as I heard of Yogi Adityanath, who wore saffron, was a part of Hindu Yuva Wahini, delivered controversial speeches, I was really upset about the decision.

> I took him as anti secular. I presumed that muslims and minors will feel so suppressed in their own country because
I took Yogi as a man who will promote a particular religion and belief.

> It would hamper the rich diversity. He ceased illegal slaughter houses and banned cow meat.

> I believe all lives are equally important. To me, it was purely a protection of Hinduism.
